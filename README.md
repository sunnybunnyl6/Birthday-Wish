# 🎉 Happy Birthday, Sanvitha! 🎂

Welcome to an interactive birthday celebration dedicated to our wonderful friend **Sanvitha**!

## ✨ Features

This beautiful birthday website includes:

### 🌅 Animated Sunset
- Realistic animated sunset scene with gradual sky color transitions
- Moving clouds and flying birds
- Gentle ocean waves reflecting sunset colors
- Dynamic lighting effects

### 🎊 Interactive Elements
- **Floating Balloons** - Watch colorful balloons float across the screen
- **Interactive Birthday Cake** - Click candles to light them, then blow them out
- **Confetti & Fireworks** - Spectacular celebratory effects triggered by buttons
- **Floating Lanterns** - Rising lanterns during the final celebration
- **Floating Hearts** - Continuously floating heart particles
- **Glowing Butterflies** - Magical butterfly animations

### 📸 Sections Included
1. **Greeting Section** - Welcome message with glowing text
2. **Memory Gallery** - Beautiful card gallery for precious memories
3. **Interactive Cake** - Fun candle-blowing experience
4. **Why Sanvitha Is Special** - Animated quality cards
5. **Heartfelt Message** - Glassmorphism-style message card
6. **Wishes Section** - Six special wishes for her future
7. **Final Message** - Grand finale with ultimate celebration

### 🎨 Design Features
- **Color Palette**: Sunset Orange, Golden Gold, Soft Pink, Lavender Purple, Midnight Blue
- **Glassmorphism Effects** - Frosted glass-like design elements
- **Smooth Scroll Animations** - Content animates as you scroll
- **Fully Responsive** - Works beautifully on all devices
- **Premium Animations** - Smooth, elegant transitions and effects
- **Soft Glowing Effects** - Ethereal, magical atmosphere

### 🎵 Audio
- Background music plays automatically (browser-dependent)
- Can be toggled with interactions

## 🚀 How to Use

1. **Open** the website in your browser
2. **Click** "Celebrate Sanvitha" to trigger initial fireworks and confetti
3. **Scroll** through the beautiful sections
4. **Interact** with the birthday cake by clicking candles
5. **Blow Out** the candles after lighting them all
6. **Scroll to the end** to witness the grand finale celebration
